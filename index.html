I am currently serving as a postdoctoral researcher under the guidance of Professor Mukesh Kumar at the University of Alabama. I am actively engaged in research at the Center for Complex Hydrosystems Research within the Department of Civil, Construction, and Environmental Engineering. My research focuses on advancing our understanding and predictive capabilities of ecohydrological processes, with particular emphasis on improving the estimation of Evapotranspiration and Soil Moisture in modern Land Surface Models. To accomplish this, I integrate advanced data analytics and modeling techniques.
