Pushpendra Raghav

Research Engineer  ·  The University of Alabama

I am a Research Engineer/Scientist working with Professor Mukesh Kumar in the Department of Civil, Construction, and Environmental Engineering at the University of Alabama. My work improves our understanding and prediction of ecohydrological processes — streamflow extremes, drought onset, evapotranspiration, and soil moisture — through synergistic use of observational data (eddy covariance flux towers, remote sensing) and models (Earth system models and data-driven machine learning). I received the Outstanding Dissertation Award from my department in 2025.
